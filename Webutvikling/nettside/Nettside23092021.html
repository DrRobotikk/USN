About the Bookworm Bookshop

Since 1933, The Bookworm Bookshop has offered rare and hard-to.find titles for the discrening reader.
The Bookworm offers:

  • Friendly, knowledgeable, and coureous help
  • Free coffee and juice for our customers
  • A well-lit reading room so you can "try before you buy"
  • Four friendly cats: Esmeralda, Catherine, Dulcina and Beatrice

Our hours are 10am to 9pm weekdays, noon to 7 on weekends.
